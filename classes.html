List of Classes Currently Available

We take pride music education as it is not only a creative output but also encourages brain development. Early training can lead to reasoning, better memory, languge skills, coordination, pattern recognition, and dicipline.

Available Monthly Classes

We offer lessons ranging anywhere from beginning to expert. These classes are built to focus on fundamental skills such as literacy, notation, rhythm, and theory.

Please note classes will not be held on national holidays*
Pricing Below is Per Month
Piano Classes $100: 1 hour courses held once a week focusing on hand posture, finger placement, and theory.
Guitar Classes $100: 1 hour courses held once a week in both acoustic and electric focusing strumming, finger placement, and even tuning
Vocals Classes $85: 45 minute courses held once a week aimed at improving singing voice by focusing on breathing pattern, posture, and tone.
Drums Classes $120: 1 hour courses held once a week focused on technique, rhythm, and song structure.
Brass Classes $150: 1 hour courses in trumpet, tuba, and trombone held twice a week focused on breathing, hand posture, and instrument care.
Jass Flute Classes $75: 35 minute weekly classes focusing on improvisation in an edgy, smooth, and soulful frequency.
Song Writing $135: 90 minute weekly classes teaching song structure as a craft to held aspiring students use music as a creative outlet.
Violin Classes $100: 1 hour weekly courses focused on techniques in holding the instrument, note reading, and melody.
Harp Classes $125: 1 hour weekly classes focusing on repertoire and ensemble playing.
Ukelele Classes $50: 30 minute weekly courses designed for those seeking to play music in a fun and accessible way. This course covers the basic of playing simple songs while also teaching rhythm and strumming techniques.
Harmonica Classes $50: 30 minute weekly classes focuses on blending notes and proper articulation.
