Meet our Faculty

Our faculty are more then just educators, they are mentors who specialize in teaching music theory, performance, composition, and history. Our faculty are professionals each with a minimum of 10,000 teaching hours within their respective diciplines. They will provide feedback and guidance to students on their musical development towards their music program's curriculum.
