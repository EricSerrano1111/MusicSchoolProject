Private Lessons

For a more curated lesson or for those with busy schedules. We offer offer private one on one sessions.

Please note classes will not be held on national holidays*
Pricing Below is Per Session
On-Site $75: 1 hour lesson at our facility with your choice of instrument.
Online $65: 1 hour lesson online through video conferencing software. We kindly ask that you log in 15 minutes before session to properly set up camera and mic.
In-Home $100: 1 hour lesson held either at your home or desired location. If needed can can supply an instrument for a small extra cost.
Special Ciscrumstances Please contact us if you have requirements outside of these private lesson options.
